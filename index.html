TENDON STRENGTH

The Angampora practice exposes the practitioner to a variety of unique and versatile training regimes. Intensity is imperative in Angampora and the physical practices stress tendon strength and mobility in favor of muscle size or strength.

Our story
Angampora is the traditional martial art form that has been shrouded in secrecy since ancient times. Indigenous to Sri Lanka its roots stretch back over 5000 years - it was once a very integral part of the Sri Lankan way of life. It is a complete and holistic system, rooted in Buddhism, Hinduism and includes elements of Helaveda, meditation, music and astrology.
