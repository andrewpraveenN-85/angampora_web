Who we are?

Ceylon Hela Art is dedicated to preserving and promoting Sri Lankan traditional heritage. This business plan outlines our Angampora training programs, which combine self-defense, fitness, mental discipline, and spiritual growth. In the future, we aim to introduce other Sri Lankan traditional arts to the world.
